# Analyse the Company

Do this before you score anything. You cannot augment what you do not
understand.

## What to capture

Write a one-page brief covering four areas. Keep it factual.

### Business model

- How does the company make money?
- Which workflows affect margin, speed, retention, or risk?
- Which teams are overloaded or error-prone?

### Workflow context

- Which workflows repeat daily or weekly?
- Where do people wait, rework, or hand off?
- Which steps are standardized, and which depend on judgment or relationships?

### Systems and data

- Which systems are the source of truth?
- Where do email, spreadsheets, or chat still run the workflow?
- What data is structured, messy, missing, or blocked?

### Constraints

- Which regulations, contracts, or internal policies matter?
- Which steps involve high-stakes judgment or relationship management?
- What would be costly or unsafe to automate incorrectly?

## Done when

- The brief fits on one page.
- The business model and bottlenecks are explicit.
- Every later workflow can link back to this context.
