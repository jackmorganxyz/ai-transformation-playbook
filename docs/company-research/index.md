# Company Research

Company research creates the context that keeps scoring honest. The point is to
understand what the business values, where workflows break, and what constraints
cannot be violated.

## What to capture

- business model and revenue mechanics
- service delivery model or operating model
- core systems of record
- compliance and customer trust constraints
- current bottlenecks, backlogs, and manual hand-offs
