# Company Research Brief

Use the brief to anchor every later scoring conversation in the same business
context.

## Research prompts

### Business model

- How does the company create revenue and margin?
- Which work directly shapes customer experience, retention, or delivery speed?
- Which functions are overloaded or error-prone today?

### Workflow context

- Which workflows repeat daily or weekly?
- Where do people wait on information, hand-offs, approvals, or rework?
- Which steps are standardized and which rely on judgment or relationship work?

### Systems and data

- Which systems are the source of truth?
- Where is work still driven by email, spreadsheets, or chat?
- What data is structured, semi-structured, or inaccessible?

### Constraints

- Which regulations, client obligations, or internal policies matter?
- Which steps involve high-stakes judgment or relationship management?
- What would be costly or unsafe to automate incorrectly?

## Output format

Capture the brief in one page, then link every scored workflow back to it.
