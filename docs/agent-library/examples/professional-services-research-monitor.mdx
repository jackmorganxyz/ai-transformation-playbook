## Problem Statement

Research teams need help with monitoring and first-pass synthesis, not fully
autonomous recommendations.

## Trigger

Run on a scheduled monitoring cadence or when a scoped brief request is opened.

## Inputs

- approved source list
- topic tags or research brief
- existing output template

## Systems/Tools

- source databases or websites
- research notes system
- brief template

## Workflow Steps

1. Retrieve new or changed source material for the scoped topic.
2. Extract evidence, quotes, and facts with citations.
3. Draft a structured brief with relevance notes and uncertainty markers.
4. Suggest next research questions where evidence is thin.

## Human Approval Points

- Analyst confirms relevance and wording.
- Engagement lead approves recommendations before client use.

## Outputs

- draft brief
- cited findings
- follow-up research questions

## Success Metrics

- reduced monitoring time
- citation accuracy
- fewer missed material updates

## Failure Modes

- weak source filtering
- unsupported summary statements
- repeated noise from unchanged content

## Security Notes

- maintain provenance for every claim
- keep client strategy context in approved workspaces

## Token-Cost Notes

- pass excerpts, not full articles, into the synthesis step
- separate retrieval from summary generation

## Deployment Mode

`Hybrid` lets the agent handle monitoring and drafting while analysts keep final
judgment.

## Prompt Skeleton

```text
Role: Research monitoring analyst

Objective: Draft a sourced brief on new developments for a defined topic.

Inputs: Approved sources, monitoring window, topic tags, prior brief template.

Constraints: Cite each material claim, flag uncertainty, and avoid strategic recommendations unless explicitly asked.

Required checks: Surface any claim that lacks direct support.

Output format: Executive summary, cited findings, implications, follow-up questions.
```
